About Me

My name is Kyle Daniels and I am thrilled to have the opportunity to tranisiton into a career in Full Stack Development. My background has been in education for the last 20 years but I have always had a love and desire for technology. I attended Hampton University in Virgina where I majored in Mathematics and marched in the Hampton University Marcing Force. In additon to my love for music and technology, I love to travel. My favorite destinations are Los Cabos and Puerto Vallarta, Mexico. My additonal hobbies include fishing, horseback riding, and chess.

During my time in education I have served as both a Math teacher and as a Math Instructional Coach. As the Math Instructional Coach I lead leaderhip teams in data analysis of Common Assessment data. I created data visualizations for teachers and school leadership, along with projecting student achievment on state test using Benchmark data and Universal Screeners.

The desire to transition from education in the tech world occured while taking a 10-week Data Analytics class at General Assembly. The idea of writing code to return data and then being able to create a story to tell about the data through data progamming language gave me the direction for my future career path.

I look forward to this journey into the tech field and learning this craft to the degree that one day I can train others that desire to pursue the same dream.
